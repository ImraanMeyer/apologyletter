Now.. Lets make some Cabbage Food.

Starting with the ingredients!

  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 30 ml sunflower oil
  • 1 kg mutton rib
  • 7 ml salt
  • 5 whole cloves
  • 2 whole allspice
  • 3 peppercorns
  • 1 medium cinnamon stick
  • 10 ml freshly ground ginger-garlic paste
  • 5 ml crushed dried red chilies
  • 1 whole green chili (optional)
  • 10 ml sugar
  • 150 ml water (please see note)
  • 1 medium cabbage, shredded finely
  • 3 medium potatoes, quartered
  • nutmeg
  1. Heat oil in heavy soup pot. Add cloves, peppercorns, and allspice. Once it starts sizzling add onions. Brown your onions nicely
  2. Add meat and simmer slowly till meat is done and well browned.
  3. Add salt, chillies, ginger and garlic paste and sugar and half cup water. Bring to a boil and simmer for about 10 minutes until flavours are well blended.
  4. Add your cabbage and potatoes and cook until vegetables are soft about 20 minutes.
  5. Sprinkle with nutmeg after serving with rice.
